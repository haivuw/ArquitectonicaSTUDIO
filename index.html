ArquitectonicaSTUDIO

Our work embraces material experimentation, research, and collaboration across all scales and mediums, with a focus towards innovative architectural projects, environments, and designed objects for cultural and commercial clients.

We approach each project with curiosity and creative inquiry, seeking ways to engage artists, writers, and makers within our community into the design process

Our work is made thoughtfully and hands-on. We work closely with our fabricators and collaborators at all stages of each project, ensuring visual and material cohesion throughout.




We are an interdisciplinary studio that weaves together art, architecture, and graphic design.

Our work embraces material experimentation, research, and collaboration across all scales and mediums, with a focus towards innovative architectural projects, environments, and designed objects for cultural and commercial clients.

We approach each project with curiosity and creative inquiry, seeking ways to engage artists, writers, and makers within our community into the design process. Our work is made thoughtfully and hands-on. We work closely with our fabricators and collaborators at all stages of each project, ensuring visual and material cohesion throughout.
